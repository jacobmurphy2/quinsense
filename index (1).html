The Problem

Dialysis is reactive. Patients pay the price.

Chronic Kidney Disease silently progresses until the kidneys fail. At End-Stage Renal Disease, patients depend on dialysis to survive ‚Äî but dialysis timing today is based on scheduled appointments, not real physiological need.

Dangerous electrolyte swings go undetected between clinic visits. Hyperkalemia ‚Äî dangerously high potassium ‚Äî is a leading cause of sudden cardiac death in dialysis patients, and it's largely preventable with earlier detection.
